About HMPSM UNPAR

Our department's journey through excellence in mathematics and natural sciences education

Our History

Established in 1960, the Department of Mathematics and Natural Sciences at Parahyangan Catholic University has been a center of excellence in scientific education and research in Indonesia.

Over the years, we have produced thousands of graduates who have made significant contributions in various scientific fields both nationally and internationally.

Our Vision & Mission

Guiding principles that shape our academic community

Vision

To become a leading center for mathematics and natural sciences education that produces competent, innovative, and ethical graduates who contribute to society.

Mission

  • Provide high-quality education in mathematics and natural sciences
  • Conduct innovative and impactful research
  • Serve the community through scientific applications
  • Develop international collaborations
